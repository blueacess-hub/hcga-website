About HCGA关于 HCGA

Our Mission

使命

Promote learning and improving the game of golf, building strong relationships among golfers of varying backgrounds and skill levels.

推动高尔夫运动学习与提升，在不同背景和水平的球友之间建立深厚友谊。

Our Vision

愿景

Motivate individuals and families to be involved in community commonweal through organized activities and daily influences.

激励个人与家庭通过有组织的活动和日常交流，积极投入社区公益事业。

Our Action

行动

Schedule outings and tournaments to promote emotional and financial help for children's education, senior care, and charities.

组织球赛与联谊活动，为社区儿童教育、老年关爱及公益组织提供支持。
